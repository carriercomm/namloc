WHY NAMLOC ?

Owner/Operator: Jamee Colman

My father was a builder, so from an age when I shouldn’t have been on a roof helping, I was his ‘Nail Boy’. This must have sparked my passion for the industry because since I can remember, I had always known what I wanted to be. I had even named my company as a child, my surname COLMAN backwards; The simplicity is proof. During work experience at college, I was offered a fulltime job and my career began. Nine years later, I am trade qualified and licensed, three year into business and my passion for the industry continues to grow. During 6 years of employment, the industry demonstrated to me, a shortage of quality workmanship and perhaps more importantly efficient communication. To me, a perfect result is achieved when the customer’s vision is accurately created via efficient communication and then applied with a high level of workmanship. Namloc Build Ltd was incorporated on 10th May 2011to provide perfect results on all of your building projects in the Wellington wide.
